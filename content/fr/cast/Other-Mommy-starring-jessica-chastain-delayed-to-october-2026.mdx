## Introduction

Universal Pictures has announced a significant delay for the highly anticipated horror film, "Other Mommy," produced by Atomic Monster. Originally set for release on May 8th, 2026, the film has been pushed back to October 9th, 2026. This new release date places it in direct competition with Paramount's "The Legend of Ang, The Last Airbender." 

## The Film's Background

"Other Mommy" is inspired by Josh Malererman's novel, "Incidents Around the Heist." The story revolves around a young girl whose family and home become the target of a malevolent force. This sinister entity seeks to make its home within her, and the film promises to take audiences on a chilling journey into the depths of her life.

## Cast and Crew

The film boasts an impressive cast, featuring Oscar-winning actress Jessica Chastain, known for her role in "It Chapter 2." Jay Duplies, Arabella Olivia Clark, and Dan Lackman also star in this chilling tale. Rob Savage, who previously directed "Host" and "The Boogeyman," is at the helm of this project. Nathan Ston, a WGA award winner, has penned the script.

## Production Details

"Other Mommy" is a collaborative effort between Atomic Monster, Blumhouse, Spin a Black Yarn, and Universal Pictures. James Juan is producing the film, with a team of executive producers including Michael Clear, Judson Scott, Macdara Keller, Savage himself, Malererman, and Ryan Lewis. Elena Glassthal oversees the project for Atomic Monster.
