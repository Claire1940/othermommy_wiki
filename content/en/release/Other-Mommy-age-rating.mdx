### The Plot and Story

The story of Other Mommy follows eight-year-old Bela, whose family is her entire world. Alongside her mother, father (Daddo), and grandmother, there is also the Other Mommy, a malevolent entity that has visited Bela for as long as she can remember and repeatedly asks to be let into her heart. As cracks appear in her parents' marriage, the presence of the Other Mommy intensifies, feeding on the emotional turmoil within the household.

#### Key Elements of the Plot

- **Bela's Perspective**: The story is told from Bela's child's-eye perspective, amplifying the dread as she tries to make sense of a threatening force only she can see.
- **The Other Mommy**: This malevolent entity is not just a ghost but a presence that seems to mirror and distort motherhood, growing in power alongside the family's emotional collapse.
- **Family Dynamics**: The entity appears to feed on the family's fractures, making the domestic drama inseparable from the supernatural threat.
